Coherence

Coherence is particularly important for all stakeholder groups. Coherence encompasses aspects such as usability, customized functionality, integration into the workflow and targeted design.

The high prioritization of coherence is derived above all from the strong emphasis on usability aspects, which is strongly emphasized in the surveys. (Gnadt et al. 2016, p. 30)

Criteria

  • Respect/Authority/Acceptance within community (qualitatively)
  • Documentations (quality & extent)
  • Feedback from surveys & tests (qualitatively)
  • User (number/growth/rate)
  • Use (intensity/duration)
  • Use (extent/width)
  • Usage of integrated databases/software/tools
  • Performance (indicators)
  • Stability (indicators)
