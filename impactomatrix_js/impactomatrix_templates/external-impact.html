    External Impact

    Has high priority in connection with visibility; advertising; accessibility; collaboration. (Gnadt et al. 2016)

    The fundamental questions are: How successful is the public relations work of a research infrastructure/environment (VRE)? How visible is the VRE in the potential user group, within the (financial) sponsors, other disciplines or in public in general? (Buddenbohm et al. 2014)
