    Dissemination

    Publication and dissemination of data, increasing user accessibility; expansion of networks.

    Basic requirement: „[A]n attractive, usable interface […]“ (Warwick et al. 2006, p. 7,8) and „a good dissemination strategy“ (Warwick 2012, p. 12).

    Criteria

    • Publication of data
    • Documentations (quality & extent)
    • Feedback from surveys & tests (qualitatively)
    • User (number/growth/rate)
    • Usage of provided results
    • Re-use of code (e.g. github branches)
    • Scientometric success (indicators)
    • Dissemination within other communities
    • Scientific publications
    • Scientific theses
